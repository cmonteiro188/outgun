TALKING TO ALL PLAYERS: Just type your message and hit ENTER.

TALKING JUST TO YOUR TEAM: Just place a dot ('.') at the very
 beginning of your message (first character).

GAME CONCEPT: You are a member of a team, either RED or BLUE,
 assigned to you at random when you connect. Your goal is to
 help your team to win, by capturing 8 (default) times the enemy
 flag. To capture the flag, a member of your team must steal
 the enemy flag and bring it to your team's flag, provided your
 flag has not been stolen already! Capiche?

HEALTH AND ENERGY: If your health drops to zero, you die. Energy
 is used for running, shooting and health protection when you
 have the SHIELD powerup (you'll know when you see it).
 Health and energy regenerate with time.

MINIMAP: On the upper-right corner of the screen is the minimap.
 It shows the contents of all rooms of the map that have at least
 one player of your team.

CHANGING TEAMS: Hit the END key to set whether you want to
 change team or not. You will change team when appropriate.

POWERUPS: If you see an item lying on the ground, grab it. It's 
 a special power-up item.

ETC.: Hit DEL to kill yourself. Hit TAB to see players'
 statistics. Hit PAGE DOWN if you want to drop the flag.
 Hit HOME to change world colors and CONTROL+HOME to restore them.
 Hit INSERT to see more messages. Hit F11 to take a screenshot.
